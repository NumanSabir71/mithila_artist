Born in Mithila, shared widely

In Nepal, the living tradition is widely known as Mithila art, centred culturally in Janakpur and the Mithila region. Across the border in India, closely related practice is often called Madhubani painting, named for Madhubani, Bihar. We honour both names and the artists who keep the vocabulary of line, colour, and story alive.

For generations, artists have painted walls and sacred surfaces with stories of nature, ritual, and devotion — knowledge passed within families and communities. Mithila Design works with makers in Nepal and partner ateliers in Madhubani, Bihar (India) to bring wall pieces, textiles, and objects to you with clear provenance and care.

What we hold to

  • Respect for regional names: Mithila art (Nepal) and Madhubani painting (India).
  • Natural pigments and thoughtful finishing wherever possible.
  • Fair, direct relationships with artists and small workshops.
